Meet the Google Developer Student Club at Ndejje university (DSC)


Developer Student Clubs (DSC) are community groups for students from any academic background in their undergraduate or graduate term. By joining a DSC, students build their professional and personal network, get access to Google developer resources, and work together to build solutions for local problems in a peer-to-peer learning environment. contact the lead of DSC Ndejje university at Face book
